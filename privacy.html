Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when using our services, including:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number from our claim check form
  • Physical address and identification information necessary to process your claim
  • Browsing data through cookies and analytics services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Search national and state databases for unclaimed funds linked to your name or business
  • Contact you regarding potential claims and recovery updates
  • Process your claim and manage our legal obligations
  • Improve our services based on usage patterns

Information Sharing

Digital Recovery Agents does NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share your information with state government agencies as necessary to process and file your unclaimed fund claims. Your privacy and security are our top priority.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure storage on protected servers
  • Limited access to personal information restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates

Google Forms

Any personal information collected through Google Forms is subject to both our privacy policy and Google's privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy practices to understand how they process your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and contractual obligations)

To exercise these rights, contact us at Contact@DigitalRecoveryAgents.com.

Cookies

Our website uses basic analytics cookies to help us understand how visitors use our site. These cookies are non-tracking and do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer, though this may affect your experience on our site.
