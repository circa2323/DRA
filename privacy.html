Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your personal information.

Effective Date: March 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Digital Recovery Agents ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and when using our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your personal information in connection with our recovery agent services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to verify your eligibility and process your recovery claim. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Identification Information

  • Full legal name
  • Date of birth
  • Social Security Number (for verification purposes only)
  • Driver's license or government-issued identification number

Contact Information

  • Mailing address
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Claim Information

  • Documentation related to your claim
  • Supporting identity verification documents
  • Correspondence regarding your claim status

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information exclusively for legitimate recovery agent services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility for recovery claims
  • Process and manage your claim recovery application
  • Communicate with you regarding your claim status and any required next steps
  • Comply with state regulatory requirements and claims verification procedures
  • Maintain accurate records of claims processing activities
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

We will never use your information for marketing purposes, selling products or services unrelated to recovery claims, or any purpose other than what is stated in this policy.

How We Share Your Information

We only share your personal information when absolutely necessary and in compliance with applicable laws. Your information may be shared with:

  • State Regulatory Agencies: We are required to share claim information with state agencies in New York, Wisconsin, Indiana, and Pennsylvania to process your recovery claim.
  • Verification Services: Third-party verification services may access limited information solely to confirm your identity and eligibility.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for any purpose. We do not share your information with any unaffiliated marketing partners or commercial entities.

Data Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Secure, password-protected databases for storing personal information
  • Limited access to personal information restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Compliance with industry-standard data protection practices
  • Secure disposal procedures for documents and records

While we strive to protect your information using reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your personal information.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request access to the personal information we have collected about you and to receive a copy of that information.

Right to Correction

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal information in our records.

Right to Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and regulatory retention requirements. Some information may need to be retained to comply with state laws regarding recovery claims.

Right to Opt-Out

You have the right to opt out of certain uses of your information. However, opting out may prevent us from processing your recovery claim.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Process and complete your recovery claim
  • Comply with state regulatory requirements and retention laws
  • Resolve any disputes or claims related to our services
  • Protect the legal interests of Digital Recovery Agents and our clients

Once your claim is resolved and applicable retention periods have elapsed, we will securely delete or anonymize your personal information. Certain information may be retained longer if required by law.
