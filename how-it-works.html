How We Recover Your Funds

Our straightforward, no-risk process makes it easy to claim money that's rightfully yours. Here's exactly what happens from start to finish.

The Recovery Process

Four simple steps between you and your unclaimed funds

1

We Find Your Funds

Our advanced research system scans state unclaimed property databases across New York, Wisconsin, Indiana, and Pennsylvania to identify surplus funds in your name. We focus exclusively on claims over $50,000, ensuring every case gets the dedicated attention it deserves. If we find funds connected to you, we'll reach out directly with specific details about the amount and source.

2

You Review & Sign Our Agreement

We'll send you a clear, easy-to-understand agreement that spells out exactly what we do and what our fee is. Our fee ranges from 10-15% of the recovered amount depending on your state's regulations. There are absolutely no upfront costs, no hidden charges, and no obligation. You only pay when you receive your funds.

3

We Handle Everything

Once you sign, our team goes to work. We file all necessary claims with the appropriate state agencies, gather required documentation, handle any legal requirements, communicate with state offices on your behalf, and navigate the entire recovery process. You don't have to deal with any paperwork, phone calls, or bureaucratic hurdles.

4

You Get Paid

Once the state approves your claim, they send you a check directly. After you receive your funds, you pay our agreed percentage. That's it. The entire process typically takes a few weeks to a few months depending on the state and complexity of the claim.
