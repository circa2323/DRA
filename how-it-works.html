How the Recovery Process Works

From discovery to payout — here's exactly what happens when we find unclaimed funds in your name.

1

We Research State Databases

We scan official government unclaimed property databases in NY, WI, IN, and PA. Our system identifies claims over $50,000 linked to your name or your family.

2

We Contact You With Details

If we find funds, we reach out via mail, email, or phone. We share the specific claim details — amount, state, and what's required to recover it.

3

You Sign a Simple Agreement

Our agreement is transparent and straightforward. Fee is 10-15% depending on your state, paid only after you receive your money. No upfront costs ever.

4

We File and Follow Up

We prepare all paperwork, gather required documentation, file claims with the state, and follow up until the claim is approved. You don't have to do anything.

5

You Receive Your Funds

The state sends your check directly to you. After you receive your money, you pay our agreed percentage. That's it.
