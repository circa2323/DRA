Common Questions

Everything you need to know about unclaimed funds recovery

What are unclaimed funds?
Unclaimed funds are money or assets held by state governments when businesses or financial institutions cannot locate the rightful owner. This includes dormant bank accounts, insurance payouts, stock dividends, tax refunds, and more.
How much do you charge?
Our fee is 10-15% of the recovered amount (depending on your state), and you only pay after you receive your funds. There are absolutely no upfront costs or hidden fees.
Can I claim the funds myself?
Yes, you can always claim funds directly from the state at no cost. However, the process can be complex and time-consuming, especially for large claims or deceased relatives' estates. We handle all the paperwork, legal requirements, and follow-up to maximize your chances of success.
Which states do you serve?
We currently serve New York, Wisconsin, Indiana, and Pennsylvania. We're expanding to additional states soon.
