Common Inquiries

Understand the mechanics of asset recovery.

What are unclaimed funds? expand_more
Unclaimed funds are financial assets that have been turned over to the state because the owner cannot be located. This includes dormant bank accounts, uncashed checks, insurance payouts, and stocks.
How much do your services cost? expand_more
We operate on a strict contingency basis. We only receive a percentage of the funds we successfully recover for you. If there is no recovery, you owe us nothing.
Is this a government agency? expand_more
No, we are a private, licensed investigative and recovery firm. While states provide search tools, we specialize in high-value, complex recoveries that often require deep forensic research and legal expertise.
How long does the process take? expand_more
Recovery times vary by state and complexity, typically ranging from 90 to 180 days. We provide regular status updates throughout the process.
