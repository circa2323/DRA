State-Specific Disclosures

Required information about fees, rights, and regulatory compliance in each jurisdiction

NY

New York

Office of the State Comptroller

Recovery Agent Fee Cap
Maximum fee of 15% of recovered funds. All fees must be compliant with New York state regulations.
Written Agreement Required
You must receive and sign a written agreement before services begin, outlining all terms and fee structures.
Right to Cancel
You have the right to cancel this agreement within the specified period. Contact the New York Office of the State Comptroller for details.
Direct Claims Available
You are not required to use a recovery agent. Funds can be claimed directly through the Comptroller's office at no cost.
WI

Wisconsin

State Treasurer

Recovery Agent Fee Cap
Maximum fee of 10% of recovered funds, per Wisconsin statute requirements.
Written Contract Required
You must receive and sign a written contract before services begin. The contract must clearly disclose all fees and terms.
Recovery Agent Registration
DRA is registered with the state of Wisconsin as a recovery agent and complies with all state registration requirements.
Direct Claims Available
You have the right to claim directly through the Wisconsin State Treasurer at no cost. Recovery agents are optional.
IN

Indiana

Attorney General

Recovery Agent Fee Cap
Maximum fee of 10% of recovered funds, in accordance with Indiana state law.
Written Agreement Required
A written agreement must be provided to you before services begin. You must understand all terms before signing.
Recovery Agent Registration
DRA is registered with the state of Indiana and complies with all requirements of the Attorney General's office.
Direct Claims Available
You may claim directly through the Indiana Attorney General's Unclaimed Property Division at no cost.
PA

Pennsylvania

Treasury Department

Recovery Agent Fee Cap
Maximum fee of 15% of recovered funds, in compliance with Pennsylvania Treasury regulations.
Written Agreement & Finder Registration
A written agreement is required before services begin. DRA must be registered as a Finder with the PA Treasury Department.
Finder Registration
DRA is registered with the Pennsylvania Treasury Department as a Finder and complies with all regulatory requirements.
Direct Claims Available
You may claim directly through the PA Treasury's Bureau of Unclaimed Property at no cost.

Important General Disclosures

1

You Are Never Required to Use a Recovery Agent

Using a recovery agent is entirely optional. All states we serve allow you to claim your unclaimed funds directly through the state agency at absolutely no cost to you.

2

All States Allow Direct Claims at No Cost

You can contact the unclaimed property office in any state directly to search for and claim funds yourself without paying any fees or commissions.

3

DRA is Licensed and Registered

Digital Recovery Agents is a licensed and registered recovery agent in all four states we serve: New York, Wisconsin, Indiana, and Pennsylvania. We comply with all state regulations and fee caps.

4

Questions About Your Rights?

If you have questions about your rights as a claimant, about recovery agents, or about the unclaimed property process in your state, contact the state agency directly. See contact information below for each state's agency.

Important Notice

By using Digital Recovery Agents, you are choosing to pay a percentage-based fee in exchange for our expertise in navigating the unclaimed property recovery process. You retain the right to cancel your agreement and claim funds directly at any time. We recommend carefully reviewing your written agreement and understanding all terms before signing.
