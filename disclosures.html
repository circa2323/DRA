General Disclosures

Digital Recovery Agents operates as a licensed finder/recovery agent in all states listed above. We comply with all applicable state regulations, licensing requirements, and statutory frameworks governing unclaimed property recovery.

Our fees never exceed state-mandated maximums. We are transparent about fee structures and provide clients with clear documentation of all costs before engagement.

Clients always have the right to file claims directly with their state at no cost. We encourage informed decision-making and provide educational resources about the state claim process.

For questions about state regulations, licensing, or our compliance status in your state, please contact us directly at Contact@DigitalRecoveryAgents.com or 980-446-8402.
